## Stakeholders
A _stakeholder_ is an individual, group or organisation that has an interest in
a product or service, can influence its decisions, or is affected by its outcomes
and/or deliverables (Stakeholder | Glossary, no date).

The stakeholders requiring consideration when developing the _Soapomorph_ are
detailed in the table below.

| Stakeholder | Interest or Influence | Action |
| ----------- | --------------------- | ------ |
| Bathroom cleaner | Needs to clean and refill it safely and efficiently | Accessible hatch for refilling and easily cleanable surfaces |
| Guests | Needs to be able to safely and easily interact with the mechanism | Zero-contact motion sensor, non-toxic surfaces, properly secured in place |
| Venue owners | Needs return on investment, safety for guests, easy disposal | Price of manufacture is fair relative to cost, disposable, sustainable materials, as above |
| Everyone | Needs environment to not be polluted | Sustainable materials, convenient to dispose of properly |
